Minesweeper Game

Instructions

  • press tab to navigate to the next element on the screen
  • press shift + tab to move back to the previous element
  • press space bar to click
  • on the game board, use arrow keys to move between cells
  • click on a cell to hear its value
  • press shift to hear numbers of unknown cells and flagged cells around a cell
  • press enter to flagged a cell
